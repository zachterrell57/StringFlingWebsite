Registration

*All registration is done online via the register button below, regardless of payment method*

In Cognito Forms, you can pay either by credit card through Paypal or check (see info below). In addition to paying the registration fee on Cognito Forms, you'll also fill out your rosters and receive a receipt. If you need a different type of invoice from that generated by Cognito Forms, please send details to Cora Cooper, corac@ksu.edu.

EARLY BIRD REGISTRATION IS OPEN NOV. 16 - DEC. 31, 2020
The registration fee is $15 during the early bird period.

REGISTRATION BETWEEN JAN. 1 - JAN. 25, 2021
Please follow the same directions above. The registration fee is increased to $20 per student.

Online Registration:
Directors should enter students' names on the appropriate rosters through the register button. Please make a copy of the completed rosters and keep it for your records.

In Cognito Forms, you can pay with either Paypal/credit card, or pay with a check by mail.

Paying by check:
Please write one check to pay for all the students, and make out the check payable to: “KANSAS STATE UNIVERSITY” (please double check that your school accountant changes any older records, as we are no longer affiliated with the Manhattan Chamber Orchestra account). Mail the check to: Dr. Cora Cooper, Kansas State University, School of Music, Theatre, and Dance, 109 McCain, Manhattan, KS 66506.

Please register and try to pay by December 10. It's more important for us to have the registration info than the payment by that date (though we appreciate both!)​
