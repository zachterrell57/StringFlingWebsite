January 30 & 31, 2021

Welcome!

Welcome to String Fling @ Home! Get ready for a fun weekend of string-centered playing workshops, games, presentations, escape rooms, and more. Curious about Mariachi? Fiddling? Love movie music? Ever wondered how string instruments are made? Maybe want to try your hand at composing music? Want to improve your fast playing, or vibrato? We’ve got it all!

Due to Covid, we’ve had to re-invent our annual String Fling—for the first time in 48 years, we won’t be able to meet together for in-person orchestras. Nevertheless, we’re going to have a great time learning about music and string playing in this online format. Read on to learn how it will work, and what the activities will be.

Join The Fun!

String students grades 4th - 9th who participate in their school orchestras or home school programs are invited to attend!

How Will String Fling Work Online?

Because of the online format this year, we have dropped the registration fee to $15 per student for those early birds who register between Nov. 16-Dec. 31, 2020. It will go up to $20 per student for registration between Jan. 1-25, 2021.

Step #1: Register your students/children by following the directions listed in the "registration page".

  • Click the "Register" link above, where you will follow the directions to register through Cognito Forms as in past years. You can pay directly in Cognito Forms with a credit card or Paypal, or send a check.
  • We will have three tracks for students this year, based on age and playing experience: First and Second year players (4th-6th grade), 7th Grade, and 8th/9th grade. Class material will be geared towards expected playing ability and social maturity.


Step #2: The email provided in the registration will be the one where Zoom links for the classes available to each track are sent.

  • If you are a teacher registering multiple students, please make sure to provide the correct links to your students based on their track.


Step #3: Zoom links will be sent out a week before the event, by Jan. 26, 2021 at the latest. They will be sent only to the email provided on the registration form.

  • Each String Fling @ Home event will have a unique link. There will be K-State string students helping to monitor the Zoom sessions throughout.
  • Please send your students the appropriate Zoom links for each track.
  • Students will be randomly sorted into “Houses” and will win (or lose) points for their house during the sessions. This information will be sent along with the Zoom links, to the email provided in the registration.


Step #4: Prepare to have fun!

  • The event will run between 10:00 a.m.- noon and 1:00 p.m.-3:00 p.m. each day, with a noon lunch hour. Students will be able to participate in eight different sessions over the two days!
  • Each session will last 30-40 minutes within the hour, leaving time for a break, or to get ready for the next one, or perhaps ask the teacher questions on the material.
  • During some hours, students will have a choice of activities. They should use the schedule to decide which one to choose!
  • There may also be pdf’s of music for some sessions that students could work on ahead of time. The pdf’s will be posted on this website, or e-mailed to teachers by request.
  • We will all come together Sunday at 2:00 for a Grand Finale with surprises and the announcement of the House Cup winner!
