Welcome!

Welcome to String Fling @ Home! Get ready for a fun weekend of string-centered playing workshops, games, presentations, escape rooms, and more. Curious about Mariachi? Fiddling? Love movie music? Ever wondered how string instruments are made? Maybe want to try your hand at composing music? Want to improve your fast playing, or vibrato? We’ve got it all! Due to Covid, we’ve had to re-invent our annual String Fling—for the first time in 48 years, we won’t be able to meet together for in-person orchestras. Nevertheless, we’re going to have a great time learning about music and string playing in this online format. Read on to learn how it will work, and what the activities will be

Who's Welcome?

Students who participate in their school orchestras and home-schooled students who study string instruments are all welcome to attend String Fling.
